The course grading will be roughly as follows:

- PrairieLearn Quizzes @ 15% (3 quizzes are dropped)
- PrairieLearn Homework @ 20%
- Three Midterms: the Midterm with the lowest score will count for 5% of your grade. The other Midterms will each count for 20% of your class grade.
- The Final exam will count for 20% of your class grade.
