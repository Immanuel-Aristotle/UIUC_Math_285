## HW1.6. Finding Derivative

Suppose that $y(t)$ solves the ordinary differential equation

$$
 \frac{dy}{dt} = \frac{ 4 - 2 y }{ 3 + 5 y } \qquad y(0)=0
$$

Find $\frac{dy}{dt}(0)$

$$\frac{dy}{dt} = \frac{4}{3}$$
