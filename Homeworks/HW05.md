## HW5.1. Solve for $y(t)$

 Find the solution to the differential equation

$$
  \frac{d^2y}{dt^2} + 6 \frac{dy}{dt}  -7 y = 0 ; \quad y(0) = -2 \quad y'(0)= 3
$$

Ans: $y(t)=- \frac{11 e^{t}}{8} - \frac{5 e^{- 7 t}}{8}$

## HW5.2. Solve for $y(t)$

 Find the solution to the differential equation

$$
  \frac{d^3y}{dt^3}  -3 \frac{d^2y}{dt^2}  -13 \frac{dy}{dt} + 15 y = 0 ; \quad y(0) = 1 \quad y'(0)=0 \quad y''(0) = 0
$$

 Hint: One root of the characteristic polynomial is -3

Ans: $y(t)=- \frac{3 e^{5 t}}{32} + \frac{15 e^{t}}{16} + \frac{5 e^{- 3 t}}{32}$
